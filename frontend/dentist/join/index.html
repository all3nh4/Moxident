How it works
Simple as a text message.
01
Patient submits a request
A patient in pain fills out a 30-second form at moxident.com with their name, zip code, and symptom.
02
You get a text
We send an SMS with the patient's name, symptom, and zip. Reply YES to accept or NO to pass.
03
Patient gets your info
If you accept, the patient receives your name, address, and phone number immediately and calls you directly.
04
You pay only on completion
A flat fee per completed appointment — billed after the visit. No shows, no charge. Ever.
