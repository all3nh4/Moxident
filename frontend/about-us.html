About Moxident

Emergency dental routing, simplified

Moxident is designed to solve a simple but urgent problem: when someone has dental pain, they often cannot find a dentist who is available right now.

Instead of calling multiple offices or waiting until morning, Moxident allows patients to submit a request and instantly notify nearby dentists who may have availability.

How it works

  • You submit your phone number and zip code
  • Moxident notifies nearby dentists in your area
  • If a dentist is available, they contact you directly

What Moxident is (and isn’t)

Moxident is a routing service that connects patients with dental providers. We do not provide medical or dental advice, and we do not schedule or manage treatment.

Why it exists

Dental emergencies don’t wait. Many patients search late at night or outside of normal business hours and end up without help.

Moxident exists to bridge that gap by connecting patients with dentists who are willing and able to take urgent cases when they arise.
