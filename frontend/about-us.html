About Moxident

Emergency dental routing, simplified

Emergency dental access is broken.

When someone has dental pain, finding a dentist who can actually see them is harder than it should be.

After hours or during busy days, most clinics are unavailable. Patients end up calling multiple offices, waiting until morning, or going without care.

In the U.S., nearly 2 million emergency room visits each year are for dental issues — most without receiving definitive treatment.

The problem isn’t a lack of dentists. It’s a lack of real-time access to ones who actually have availability.

What Moxident does

Moxident connects patients with dentists who have the ability to take urgent cases.

When a patient submits a request, we notify nearby dentists in our network who have indicated they can handle emergency cases. If a clinic is available, they can respond and contact the patient directly.

No directories. No calling around. No waiting for offices to open.

How it works

  • You submit your phone number and zip code
  • Moxident notifies nearby dentists in the network
  • If a clinic is available, they contact you directly

What Moxident is (and isn’t)

Moxident is a routing service that connects patients with dental providers based on availability.

We do not provide medical or dental advice. We do not schedule or manage treatment. All care decisions are handled directly between patients and providers.

Why it exists

Dental emergencies don’t follow business hours. Patients need a way to reach care when it’s actually needed — not just when clinics are open.

At the same time, dental offices have cancellations, gaps, and same-day openings that often go unused.

Moxident exists to connect those two sides in real time.
